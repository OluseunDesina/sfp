Personal Information We Collect About You

We strive to provide secure, relevant and efficient services to provide a great user experience. To do so, we may collect your personal information as described in this policy. Personal information is defined as information that can or does identify you as an individual or otherwise as contained in the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019 and any amendments thereto.

The types of personal information we may collect include, but are not limited to:

  1. Profile & Contact Data (e.g. your first name, last name, telephone number, corporate e-mail address, personal e-mail address, password). This information is generally collected from you or your employer;
  2. Employer Data (e.g. your employer name, office address, employees). This information is generally collected from your employer.
  3. Transaction Data(e.g. the history of your purchases and your order details. This includes:
    • what you purchased
    • your order mode
    • date, time of purchase
    • amount paid for order
    • payment method (Company or personal)
    • in-App notes

    This information is generally collected from you.

  4. Browsing Data  (e.g. access dates and times, which features you used, duration of visit, crashes). This information is generally collected from you or third parties.
  5. Survey Data  (e.g. if you choose to participate in one of our User surveys or research initiatives, we collect your answers and use the information and survey results to help us understand our users and tailor our service offerings). This information is generally collected from you.

Legal rights

  • Request correction of your personal data.
  • Request erasure of your personal data.
  • Object to processing of your personal data.

  • Request transfer of your personal data.
